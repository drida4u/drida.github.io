About Us

Drida is a non-profit organization that offers various courses related to meditation, yoga, fitness with key focus on women empowerment. It also provides free education to children in need, and aims to create a healthy and peaceful society.

Smitha Haridas

Founder, Drida

Formerly known as PV, we rebranded to Drida in 2025 — a name that reflects our deeper roots and evolving mission. The journey continues.
