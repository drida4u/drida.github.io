Trips & Events

🛕 Pilgrimages

Drida organises group pilgrimages to powerful spiritual destinations across India. To register interest for upcoming pilgrimages, send us a WhatsApp message.

Pilgrimage

Adikailash Yatra

A divine trek to Adikailash — the ancient abode of Lord Shiva nestled in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand. A journey of faith, endurance and inner transformation.

📍 Uttarakhand Ladies Only

Kasardevi Yatra

A spiritually charged journey to Kasardevi temple in Almora, with visits to the ancient Jageswar Temples and the Katarmal Sun Temple — sacred sites of immense cosmic energy.

📍 Almora, Uttarakhand Ladies Only

Maha Kumbh Mela — Prayagraj

The world's largest spiritual gathering. Drida joined the sacred Maha Kumbh 2025 at Prayagraj — taking a holy dip at the Triveni Sangam and experiencing the divine energy of millions of devotees.

Mookambika Temple, Kollur

A deeply devotional visit to the sacred Mookambika Devi temple in Kollur, one of the most revered Shakti shrines of South India, nestled at the foothills of the Western Ghats.

📍 Kollur, Karnataka

Rameshwaram

A sacred pilgrimage to Rameshwaram — one of the four dhams — where the land meets the sea and where Lord Rama worshipped Shiva. Includes holy baths in the sacred theerthams.

📍 Tamil Nadu

Kottiyoor Mahashivaratri

The ancient Kottiyoor temple in Kannur — open only for 28 days a year — is home to a unique Shiva ritual performed in an open-air forest shrine with a natural stream.

📍 Kannur, Kerala
